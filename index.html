Why Choose GO2SNOW?

Here at GO2SNOW, we believe in skiing on your terms; wherever you want to fly from, however long you want to go for, we'll get you to the mountains in the quickest, easiest and most convenient way possible. We’ve been selling ski holidays and ski multi-centre breaks for over 10 years and are here to guide you, to inspire you, and to tailor a ski break to your precise needs.

If you don't have time or holiday to go skiing for a whole week or you're looking for a few extra days in the mountains this winter, our hand-picked collection of over 40 hotels, catered ski chalets and self-catering apartments across all major Canadian ski resorts, all of which are available for ski weekends - could be just what you're looking for. Plus, a large number of our resorts have been chosen for their proximity to the airport as we know how important a short transfer is when you've only got a few days on the slopes.

We believe in being honest and open in everything we do in order to give you the best experience possible, so if you're not sure about something or can't find exactly what you're looking for, let us know, we know a thing or two about ski holidays.
